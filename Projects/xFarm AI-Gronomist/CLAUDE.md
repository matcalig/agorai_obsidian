# Project: xFarm AI-Gronomist

## Overview
Agorai + **xFarm Technologies** collaboration ("AI-Gronomist") — one of Agorai's four use cases (alongside ARM/Fincantieri, SHIELD/flood-FVG, FVG C.A.R.E./health). A **coffee** use case in **Brazil**: build an AI agronomist that gives farmers actionable recommendations (yield prediction, crop protection, harvest timing), validated through **customer discovery with Illy's network of coffee producers**.

Currently in the **discovery phase** under a signed MoU. Positive discovery → contracted **Phase 2**.

## Discovery strategy
- **Filter → interview:** short **WhatsApp survey** to gauge interest/availability, narrow to **7–10 interviews**. Deliberately include a **diverse range of farm sizes** (not just the biggest) to gauge digital & data readiness.
- **Farmer list:** Elie holds a large list and qualifies candidates; Agorai/xFarm request "ready to talk" candidates.
- **Two-step deck:** send a longer intro presentation first, a shorter reminder before the interview. Deck exists, **needs Portuguese translation** (Brazilian team).
- **Interview format — UNRESOLVED:** in-person vs online. xFarm/Google assumed online (more flexible); Matteo assumed in-person. **Matteo to consult Alex and decide.**
- **Conducted in Portuguese**, localized for Brazil (André/Anilton review questions).

## Interview protocol
A detailed **AgorAI Farmer Interview Protocol** (coffee/Brazil, revised Jul 2026) exists in Drive — 9 sections, every question tagged `[Core]` or conditional. Covers: screening, profiling, operations/irrigation, records & **EUDR** (deadline postponed to **30 Dec 2026**), crop protection & disease priorities (ferrugem, cercosporiose, broca, bicho-mineiro), health/chemical use, harvest & yield prediction, mechanisation, technology/AI/robotics, business. Built on Nakul's draft, revised by Matteo. Local units: **sacas/hectare, alqueire**.
